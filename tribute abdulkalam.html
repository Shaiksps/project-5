He was president of India from 2002 to 2007 and was known as the 'Missile Man of India' for his work in the development of ballistic missile technology.

From 1992 to 1999, Kalam was the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister and the Secretary of the DRDO. Kalam was the Chief Project Coordinator during the Pokhran II tests, and we are very proud to be a part of his great achievements.
